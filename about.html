ABOUT ME

I currently work as a software engineer at AppNeta.

A musician and a bookworm, I graduated from Harvard in 2010 with a joint degree in English and Music and entered the world of publishing as a market researcher at Scholastic. After conducting research for the launch of Storia (an e-reading app), curiosity about the software development process led me to transition into a Product Management role.

As a PM, the experience of successfully deploying several education apps internationally and working closely with distributed teams of engineers drove my desire to build applications myself. I started teaching myself Python in my free time, discovered I loved it, and took the leap to attend Hackbright Academy and devote myself to programming full-time.

I love solving problems and building things with code, and I am constantly working to learn new technologies and grow my engineering skills. I am particularly fascinated by the parallels between programming and music composition and enjoy writing beautifully composed code.
