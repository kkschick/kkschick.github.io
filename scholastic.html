WORK AT SCHOLASTIC

I worked at Scholastic for four years on a variety of software products—first as a market researcher, and then as a product manager.

As a product manager at Scholastic, I managed the development of Scholastic Learning Zone—a platform for educational apps and classroom management tools, designed specifically for the international market. I also managed the development of all the educational apps, successfully launching three new apps on a tightly compressed timeline and budget: Scholastic Literacy Pro (LitPro), Literacy Pro Library, and Scholastic Reading Inventory.

As a market researcher, I worked on the launch of Storia, an e-reading app, developing and executing a research plan around the launch. This included usability testing, a pilot testing program, claims testing, focus groups, and quantitative tracking studies.
